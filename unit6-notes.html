WHO IS RIGHT? To solve (x − 4)² − 25 = 0, Jeff takes square roots and Maria factors. Who is correct?
Both work. Square roots: (x−4)² = 25 → x − 4 = ±5 → x = 9 or −1. Factoring (difference of squares): (x−4+5)(x−4−5) = (x+1)(x−9) → x = −1 or 9. Same answers.
